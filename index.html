Our Mission

"Our vision for Tech is to create a student government that continually supports all of the passions and enterprises of students, and advocate those interests to the Georgia Tech administration. Our promise is that we will work relentlessly to implement helpful, sustainable, and creative programs, so that while students are at Tech, they will have the opportunity to self-actualize as world-renowned scholars and feel prepared to enter the world as stewards of progress and service. We believe that SGA can best serve the Georgia Tech community by empowering others instead of serving ourselves, and we pledge to create an administration where we truly listen to what students want and need.
